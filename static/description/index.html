Donation Management

Accept and manage online donations with recurring payments

Features

Online Donation Forms

Beautiful, responsive donation forms integrated with your Odoo website. Donors can easily select amounts, choose campaigns, and complete payments.

Recurring Donations

Accept monthly, quarterly, or yearly recurring donations with automatic payment processing using saved payment tokens.

Payment Provider Integration

Works seamlessly with any Odoo payment provider - Stripe, PayPal, Authorize.net, and more. Secure payment token storage for recurring donations.

Donor Portal

Donors can log in to view their complete donation history, manage recurring donations, and download tax receipts.

Campaign Management

Create fundraising campaigns with goals and track progress. View detailed analytics on campaign performance.

Automated Processing

Automatic recurring donation processing with failure handling and donor notifications. Tax receipt generation included.

Easy to Use

Simple setup and configuration. Works with your existing payment providers. No technical knowledge required to get started.
